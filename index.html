Visit Bengaluru

Savor culture and energy in this dynamic tech-and-heritage hub nestled near the heart of Bengaluru.

Top three activities to do at Bengaluru

Step into Royal Grandeur

Wander through the regal halls of Bangalore Palace, where Tudor-style architecture meets opulent decor. Its intricate wood carvings, vintage furniture, and expansive grounds paint a vivid picture of Bengaluru's royal heritage.

Nature's Calm in the City's Heart

Escape into the green sanctuary of Cubbon Park, a sprawling 300-acre retreat nestled in the heart of Bengaluru. With its tree-lined paths, colonial-era buildings, and gentle ambience, it's a favorite for walkers, photographers, and anyone seeking calm.

Thrills and Splashes for All Ages

Experience excitement at Wonderla Amusement Park, Bengaluru's adrenaline capital on Mysore Road. Whether you're diving into water rides or braving gravity-defying coasters, this park guarantees high-energy fun from dawn to dusk.
